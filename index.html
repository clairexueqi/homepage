About Me

I'm Song Li, a fourth-year Ph.D. student at the Johns Hopkins University majoring in Computer Science. I'm advised by Dr. Yinzhi Cao. My research interests are primarily focused on security areas including web security, system security, program analysis, etc. I use to work on web tracking and now I'm focusing on static/dynamic analysis, trying to solve real-world challenging problems like increasing the accuracy and efficiency of vulnerability detection.
